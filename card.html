Beartrocious

Beartrocious is a large, fast, and friendly bear.

  • Scientific Name: Ursidae
  • Average Length: 2.5m / 8.2 ft
  • Life Span: 30 years
  • Habitat: Forest

Although clumsy, bears can move fast, through dense cover that would impede a human or a horse. Natural food is their preference. They will eat human food if available.
